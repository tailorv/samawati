    • Our Story



    We are on a mission to build a care system that prioritises wellness by helping people adopt and sustain healthy lifestyles.

    At one point in our lives we get to a stage where we are responsible for the health and wellbeing of ourselves, our family members, friends, colleagues and even that of our neighbours. This realisation inspired the start of Samawati.

    We have started by getting people to exercise regularly to prevent chronic diseases, build mental resilience and reduce the rising cost of healthcare while improving quality of life.

    Samawati exists to increase the accessibility and participation of wellness focussed activities.
